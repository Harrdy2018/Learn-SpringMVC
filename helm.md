## helm使用
```shell
## https://get.helm.sh/helm-v3.9.0-linux-amd64.tar.gz

mkdir -p /opt/sw/helm/install
cp helm-v3.9.0-linux-amd64.tar.gz /opt/sw/helm/install
cd /opt/sw/helm/install
tar -xzvf helm-v3.9.0-linux-amd64.tar.gz
cp ./linux-amd64/helm /usr/local/bin/
whereis helm
```
